The event

The Bitcoin Research Day is dedicated to academic research that benefits Bitcoin. The event will have two parts: presentations and open table discussions. There will be a list of invited speakers. Attendees should apply. The goals of this day are the following:

  • Bring together researchers and developers
  • Initiate collaborations
  • Recognize outstanding work

Invited speakers and participants:

  • Leading academics that have been studying Bitcoin (Dan Boneh, Victor Shoup, Ittay Eyal, Neha Nerula, Shafi Goldwasser, Eli Ben Sasson, etc.)
  • Research oriented developers (Pieter Wuille, Tim Ruffing, Tadge Dryja, Ethan Heilman, Yehuda Lindel, Blue Matt, etc.)
  • PhD students and earlier-career academics (post docs, young faculty)
